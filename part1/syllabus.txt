Speech Understanding Course Syllabus - Technical Vocabulary

Module 1: Fundamentals of Speech Signals
Speech production, phonemes, phonetics, phonology, articulatory features,
source-filter model, glottal pulse, formants, pitch, fundamental frequency,
voicing, stochastic signal, stationarity, short-time analysis, windowing,
Hamming window, Hann window, pre-emphasis, framing, overlap-add.

Module 2: Feature Extraction
Mel-frequency cepstral coefficients, MFCC, cepstrum, quefrency, liftering,
Linear Predictive Coding, LPC, perceptual linear prediction, PLP, log filterbank,
Linear Frequency Cepstral Coefficients, LFCC, Constant-Q Cepstral Coefficients, CQCC,
delta features, delta-delta, cepstral mean normalization, CMVN, spectrogram,
mel-spectrogram, chroma features, pitch contour, F0, jitter, shimmer, zero-crossing rate.

Module 3: Acoustic Modeling
Hidden Markov Model, HMM, Gaussian Mixture Model, GMM, Viterbi algorithm,
Baum-Welch, forward-backward algorithm, maximum likelihood estimation,
expectation maximization, EM algorithm, acoustic model, phoneme recognition,
triphone, senone, decision tree clustering.

Module 4: Deep Learning for Speech
Recurrent neural network, RNN, LSTM, GRU, bidirectional LSTM, BiLSTM,
connectionist temporal classification, CTC, attention mechanism, transformer,
self-attention, multi-head attention, encoder, decoder, encoder-decoder,
conformer, wav2vec, HuBERT, Whisper, Wav2Vec 2.0, XLS-R, beam search,
greedy decoding, logit bias, constrained decoding, n-gram language model,
shallow fusion, deep fusion, rescoring.

Module 5: Speaker and Language
Speaker recognition, speaker verification, speaker identification,
d-vector, x-vector, i-vector, GE2E loss, generalized end-to-end,
speaker embedding, cosine similarity, PLDA, probabilistic linear discriminant analysis,
language identification, LID, code-switching, Hinglish, code-mixed speech,
zero-shot, few-shot, cross-lingual transfer.

Module 6: Speech Synthesis and TTS
Text-to-speech, TTS, Tacotron, FastSpeech, WaveNet, WaveGlow, HiFi-GAN,
vocoder, mel-spectrogram decoder, VITS, YourTTS, Meta MMS, duration predictor,
prosody, intonation, stress, rhythm, fundamental frequency contour,
energy contour, dynamic time warping, DTW, prosody transfer, voice cloning,
zero-shot voice cloning, speaker conditioning.

Module 7: Robustness
Noise reduction, denoising, spectral subtraction, DeepFilterNet, Wiener filter,
reverberation, dereverberation, data augmentation, SpecAugment, adversarial example,
adversarial perturbation, fast gradient sign method, FGSM, signal-to-noise ratio, SNR,
anti-spoofing, countermeasure, bona fide, spoofed, equal error rate, EER,
ASVspoof, presentation attack.

Module 8: Evaluation
Word error rate, WER, character error rate, CER, mel-cepstral distortion, MCD,
Perceptual Evaluation of Speech Quality, PESQ, short-time objective intelligibility, STOI,
mean opinion score, MOS, F1 score, precision, recall, confusion matrix, ROC curve,
detection error trade-off, DET.

Common Hinglish terms: stochastic, cepstrum, phoneme, prosody, formant,
embedding, transformer, attention, convolution, backpropagation, gradient descent,
softmax, sigmoid, cross-entropy, regularization, overfitting, batch normalization.
